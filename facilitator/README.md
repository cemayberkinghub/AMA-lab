# Facilitator Support

This folder is for visible-safe delivery support. It is separated from `class\` so students have a clean path, but it does not depend on secrecy. Assume students may browse it.

## Use these files

- [`run-of-show.md`](run-of-show.md) - timing, sequence, compression, and debrief anchors.
- [`deck-flow.md`](deck-flow.md) - slide/story flow, presenter notes, transitions, and compression rules.
- [`environment-fallback.md`](environment-fallback.md) - readiness checks, fallback choices, and recovery language.
- [`delivery-guidance.md`](delivery-guidance.md) - stance, interventions, and facilitation principles.

## Visible-safe rule

Do not put private participant judgments, sensitive customer notes, hidden answer keys, credentials, access details, or content that would undermine class participation here. Calibration examples may be discussed, but they should be framed as examples rather than secrets.

## Separation from student flow

Students start in [`class\README.md`](../class/README.md). Facilitator files support delivery, pacing, and fallback decisions; they are not required for normal student participation.
